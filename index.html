I’m an Audio Programmer and entrepreneur.

Need help bringing an audio post-production or music software to life? Get in touch.

I design audio software, consult in the post-production tech space, and co-founded Audiokids in 2020—a company that delivers high-quality tools for audio post-production professionals.

My journey with Audiokids taught me how to bootstrap a startup and build audio software from the ground up, managing every step along the way. This was made possible also by my experience as a re-recording mixer, a role I’ve held since 2016.

I specialized in sound for film at Italy's National Film Institute (Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia), and I taught myself programming to create the software solutions that didn’t exist when I worked in the industry. This led to founding Audiokids.

Beyond work, I’m passionate about music, cinema, literature, and cooking.

Agile methodologies, clean code, and test-driven development are my go-tos for bringing ideas to reality.
