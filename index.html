Audio Programmer & entrepreneur

Specializing in real-time audio processing, C/C++ development, and audio plugin architecture for professional post-production workflows. Need help bringing audio software to life? Get in touch.

Professional Background

I design and develop audio software solutions, consult in the post-production tech space, and co-founded Audiokids in 2020—a company delivering high-performance tools for audio post-production professionals using the JUCE framework and serving clients worldwide.

My approach combines deep industry knowledge from 5+ years as a re-recording mixer with robust software engineering practices. This unique perspective allows me to build tools that solve real workflow challenges with user-centered design and intuitive interfaces tailored to professional needs.

Education & Origins

I specialized in sound for film at Italy's National Film Institute (Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia). When the industry lacked the specific tools needed for advanced post-production workflows, I taught myself programming to bridge that gap—ultimately leading to founding Audiokids.

Development Philosophy

I leverage user-centered design and clean code practices to deliver robust, maintainable audio applications that integrate seamlessly into professional workflows.
